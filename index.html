About

I am a master's student on the Erasmus Mundus IPCVai programme, a competitive joint degree spanning Pázmány Péter Catholic University, Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, and Université de Bordeaux.

My background is in biomedical engineering (Universidad de los Andes, Colombia), which gives me an unusual angle on vision problems — I care about measurement, uncertainty, and what a model is actually encoding, not just whether its outputs look plausible.

My current work sits at the intersection of neural rendering, 3D reconstruction, and deep learning fairness. I am looking for Computer Vision internships in Germany from early 2027.

Selected work

01 — TRDP · 2025–2026

Novel View Synthesis:
3DGS vs. Instant-NGP

Systematic comparison of 3D Gaussian Splatting and Instant-NGP across four scene types — including a 200° FOV fisheye capture — inside the Nerfstudio framework.

+22 dB PSNR · 37–74 FPS vs 0.1 FPS
PyTorch Nerfstudio COLMAP CUDA
↗ 02 — IEEE ICIP 2026 · Tampere

Urban Re-ID Challenge

Cross-camera matching of urban infrastructure across a structural domain gap — training on forward cameras, evaluation on a single backward camera with no overlapping pairs.

🏆 5th place · mAP 0.175 · +71.7% over baseline
PyTorch ViT-Large DANN Re-ID
↗ 03 — PPCU · 2025–2026

Gender Bias Audit in Vision-Language Models

Logit-level audit of LLaVA-1.5-7B for binary gender bias toward non-binary identities. Isolated the CLIP visual encoder as the source of misgendering via text-only ablation.

Vocabulary collapse, not misclassification
LLaVA CLIP HuggingFace UMAP
